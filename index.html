TL;DR: Nico's personal internet index

The classic google search is about to disappear. I liked it. I grew up with it. This will be my replacement as I live in a fairly small corner of the internet.

Bueno dot com was obviously taken. So we’re most the other domain names. I found bueno.fyi. At its inception it’s only going to be a bunch of link. My personal bookmarks. A launchpad. Since I dislike extension and dedicated app, a simple webpage seems appropriate.

The idea of providing a node for the internet pleases me. It goes against the centralized dynamics of the moment. May this be my modest contribution to the indie web or remain a personal tool for the preservation of sanity, spring me a few moment in AI-land. Although not all things listed will be independents. I’ll leave it up to my test of if integrity to define what I list. So it’s somehow based on trust and randomness.
